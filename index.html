Solar in Arkansas

While we service customers around the nation
We are proud to call the natural state our home

Unlike some other states there are no state-based or utility-based incentives for installing solar in the Arkansas. On the bright side Arkansas receives more sunlight then most areas in the US, so a solar system in Arkansas can produce more energy then sytems with access to less sunlight.

Also, Arkansas has a state-widde net metering policy, so residents who install solar on their homes or buisnesses will receive compensation for the excess energy their solar system produces. Aditionally, Arkansas residents can still take advantage of the 26% federal tax credit.
